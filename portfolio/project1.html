GITHUB POETRY PROJECT

For this project, "theif" was the last phrase I texted someone. Hence, I chose the title of my Github Poetry Project to be "A theif" and have written a poetry on it.

The Theif

Two bodies were lying on the dust

The bodies of brothers who were guiltless

How visiously killed, that is speechless

Neither the theif nor the rapist they were

Just 2 innocent neive guys they were

Beaten by sticks, kicked by the feet

Drenched in blood like a red sheet
